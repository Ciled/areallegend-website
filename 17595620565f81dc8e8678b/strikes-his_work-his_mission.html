What is a Strike? And, how can I get one?
Strikes are the way we have to count the number of server rules that a user has disrespected, they are achieved when an individual makes a violation of the rules that we have defined (obviously)
They are cumulative for a period of 84 hours (a half week) , so after that time the Strike will be revoked. Depending on the severity of the fault, that amount of time could be lengthened or shortened

What can happen if I have many Strikes?
3 Strikes = Mute 84 hours (A half week)
5 Strikes = Mute 168 hours (A week)
8 Strikes = TEMP BAN 168 hours (A week)
​10 Strikes = TEMP BAN 192 hours (A week and a day)

Penalties may vary depending on various factors. among them are: Severity of the situation (because of the broken rule), the rank of the offender, the context of the situation, etc. What appears there are references to the most possible to happen, each individual case is unique. However, despite so many factors, it is quite possible that those penalties are used as a basis or as received penalty, but I repeat, it could vary.
